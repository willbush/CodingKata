Hex to Nx8 bitmap
======

Solution to http://www.reddit.com/r/dailyprogrammer/comments/2ao99p/7142014_challenge_171_easy_hex_to_8x8_bitmap/

I decided not to limit the rows to 8 hence (Nx8), but the columns are limited to 8.
